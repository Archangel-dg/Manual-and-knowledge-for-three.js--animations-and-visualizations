17_CINEMATIC_SCENE_TEMPLATES.md

Three.js Cinematic Scene Templates

Purpose

This document defines reusable production templates for creating premium Three.js scenes.

The goal is to provide proven structures for:

* product showcases
* game intros
* casino environments
* fantasy worlds
* futuristic interfaces
* interactive experiences

A strong scene begins with a strong structure.

⸻

1. Cinematic Reveal Template

Purpose

Used for:

* product launches
* NFT reveals
* premium objects
* hero animations

⸻

Scene Structure

Cinematic Reveal
├── Dark Environment
├── Hero Object
├── Controlled Lighting
├── Camera Movement
├── Atmosphere
└── Final Highlight

⸻

Visual Direction

Start:

Object hidden.

Low light.

Slow movement.

⸻

Build-up:

Camera approaches.

Light reveals details.

Material highlights appear.

⸻

Final:

Object fully visible.

Effects activate.

Logo or information appears.

⸻

Camera

Recommended:

Slow dolly movement.

Small rotation.

Depth of field.

⸻

Lighting

Use:

* key light
* rim light
* environment reflections

⸻

Effects

Possible:

* particles
* bloom
* fog
* subtle energy

⸻

12. Final Cinematic Rule

A cinematic scene is not created by adding more effects.

It is created by controlling:

* composition
* timing
* light
* movement
* attention

Professional scenes guide the viewer’s emotions.
